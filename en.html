Danielle, Alain and their family look forward to meet you in Dordogne in this house and are available to help you during all your stay.

Near a ridge, a little way away of the town of La Chapelle Gonaguet, the country cottage of Pavillon will offer you the quietness of the countryside.

This country cottage is near the main farmhouse, which was an ancient hunting lodge of the bishops of Périgueux.

We will be please to provide you information about the surrounding area's tourist attractions in Périgord and help you move.
